Thankfully, Infinite Painter has a tool that duplicates the strokes of my stylus. So, I set it to produce five sets of strokes. With each successive layer my amulet took shape, but it was lacking something. I wanted to insert some gemstones into the arms and center of my amulet.<!-- more --> When I finished the drawing, I needed to place it onto something. After several false starts, I turned to a simple wooden background.
